About

Founded by Mark Feldman and Leon Nahon in 1996, Nahon Feldman and Associates cc quickly became known as a trusted and friendly firm for financial advice and investments. Recently, the company re-branded as Onevest Wealth cc to better reflect the broad array of financial services offered.
Onevest Wealth is trusted by hundreds of clients for a variety of financial services, including:

  • Financial investments
  • Long-term insurance
  • Medical aids
  • Estate planning
  • Group benefits
  • General financial advice
